Trump: "America Will Never Be A Socialist Country"; "We Were Born Free And We Will Stay Free"

|
Posted By Ian Schwartz
On Date February 5, 2019

President Trump said the United States will never become a socialist country in his 2019 State of the Union address.

"We stand with the Venezuelan people in their noble quest for freedom â and we condemn the brutality of the Maduro regime, whose socialist policies have turned that nation from being the wealthiest in South America into a state of abject poverty and despair," Trump said Tuesday night.
