'Abolition of civil rights' America's great achievement: Trump
short by Nandini Sinha / 09:38 pm on 08 Feb 2019,Friday
US President Donald Trump accidentally told a gathering of primarily religious leaders that one of America's greatest achievements is the "abolition of civil rights". The transcript released later showed Trump was supposed to say America's successes range "from gaining independence, to abolition [of slavery], to civil rights". He also hailed extending the vote for women as America's achievement.
