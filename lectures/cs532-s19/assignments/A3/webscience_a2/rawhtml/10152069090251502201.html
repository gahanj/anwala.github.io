    Who Said It: Trump or Gotti?

    By RALPH BLUMENTHAL AUG. 24, 2018

    Opinion Who Said It: Trump or Gotti?
    By Ralph Blumenthal
    Mr. Blumenthal is a former New York Times reporter and he wrote the foreword to “The Gotti Tapes.”

    President Trump’s language has drawn comparisons to that of the Mafia boss John Gotti. Reuters; Al Drogo for The New York Times

    With President Trump and his associates facing mounting legal trouble, he has started using vocabulary that sounds increasingly similar to that of John Gotti, who led the Mafia’s dominant Gambino family before his 1992 conviction on murder and racketeering charges. Just how similar? See if you can tell the difference.

    1 of 17
    “You see, he’s one of – excuse the expression, he’s ‘a friend of ours.’ Continental slime, that’s what he is.”
    Trump
    Gotti
    Correct! The correct answer was Gotti.

    Trump
