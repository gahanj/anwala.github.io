Flashback: Trump and shock jock Don Imus trade jokes about American Indian tribe

June 17, 2016

Don­ald Trump, ap­pear­ing on the Don Imus ra­dio show in 1993, mocked a Con­necti­c­ut tribe that had opened the Fox­woods casino. At the time, Trump op­er­ated three casi­nos in At­lantic City and was fight­ing what he saw as un­fair com­pet­i­tion from Nat­ive Amer­ic­an tribes.
