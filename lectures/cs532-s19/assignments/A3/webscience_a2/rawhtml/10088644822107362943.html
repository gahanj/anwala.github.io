Pediatric cancer: Dr. Andrew Kung interview on C-SPAN

by News Desk
February 9, 2019
Non-Communicable Health News
No Comments

Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center Department of Pediatrics Chair and Pediatric Oncologist, Dr. Andrew Kung was on C-SPAN Saturday morning to discuss pediatric cancer and President Trump’s call to boost pediatric cancer research funding by $500 million over the next decade.

Dr. Kung also took calls from the audience.

Great discussion.
