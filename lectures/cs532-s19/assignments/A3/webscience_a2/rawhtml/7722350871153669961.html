Journalists accused trump of feeding US Alaska Pollock from Russia

Posted by admin — 10/02/2019 in Политика 0 0
Tweet on Twitter Share on Facebook Google+ Pinterest

The policy of the President of the United States Donald trump has led to a rise in the share of Russian Pollock in the United States, according to CNN.

In 2017, almost half of the fish sticks, made in USA, was from the Russian Alaska Pollock. The reason for this is the pricing policy where the Russian fish became cheaper caught the Americans off the coast of Alaska.

Hope fishermen from the United States to the rejection of the price advantage Russian producers of Alaska Pollock on the American were defeated, said the publication.
