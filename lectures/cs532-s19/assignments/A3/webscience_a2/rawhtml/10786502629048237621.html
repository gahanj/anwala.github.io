Published on Jan 18, 2019

Are you aware that most of the drugs coming into the United States…are entering through ports of entry?’ — Kamala Harris pulled all the receipts on AG pick William Barr when asking him about his views on the wall.
» Subscribe to NowThis: http://go.nowth.is/News_Subscribe

Senator Kamala Harris squared off against President Trump's attorney general nominee William Barr at the attorney general confirmation hearing. Barr is in line to replace Jeff Sessions. Kamala Harris wanted to know Barr's position on a border wall. The Trump administration's lie of choice has been that border drugs are flowing across the border away from ports of entry. The truth is that people are smuggling drugs most through legal ports of entry. In the William Barr testimony, the attorney general hopeful spouted that same border security lie. Is this a preview of Kamala Harris 2020?
