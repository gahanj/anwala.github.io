*Hollywood would like America to believe it only reflects our nation’s pulse. That being the case, 76 percent of Americans approved of this speech by that second-or-third grader destroying our economy as a pawn of Russia because he wants to kill pregnant women.*
By Brent Bozell and Tim Graham ~
The liberal desire to rebut President Trump is so overwhelming that they’re now  “fact-checking” him live or the moment his last sentence is completed. The State of the Union has an Official Democrat Politician Rebuttal, but why bother? They need only give links to national “news” sites since the “news” anchors will offer their “context” as soon as the speech is over.
Or better yet, leave it to the “comedians.”
One could easily consider the late-night talk shows to be the Democrat rebuttal. Start with The Daily Show on Comedy Central, where Trevor Noah quipped Trump’s theme was “we’re all going to die, Mexican style…This part of his speech was so scary he should have just done it with a flashlight under his chin.”
Trump was dismissed as dumber than a fifth-grader. “We can all agree that he read really well,” Noah announced…
