Trump: ‘Abolition of civil rights’ led by people of faith
During U.S. President Donald Trump’s speech at the annual National Prayer Breakfast on Thursday morning, he said that some of America’s greatest accomplishments, like the “abolition of civil rights,” have been led by people of faith.
