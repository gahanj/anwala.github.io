Donald Trump Calls On Congress To Ban Late-Term Abortions
‘These are living, feeling, beautiful babies who will never get to share their love and their dreams with the world’
POTUS called on Congress to pass legislation prohibiting these unnecessary abortions in the wake of laws recently pushed in New York and Virginia.
“All children, born and unborn, are made in the holy image of God,” Trump explained.
