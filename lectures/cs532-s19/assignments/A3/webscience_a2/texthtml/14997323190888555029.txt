White House Press Secretary Sarah Sanders said President Trump "closed the deal" for Indiana businessman Mike Braun, who Fox News projects to unseat Democratic Sen. Joe Donnelly on Tuesday.
"This was a candidate that a lot of people projected to lose," Sanders said.
"The president went in and closed the deal for Mike Braun last night," she said.
