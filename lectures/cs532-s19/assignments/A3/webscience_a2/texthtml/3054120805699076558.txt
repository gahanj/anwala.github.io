Oprah’s guru, Marianne Williamson, gears up to defeat Donald Trump with love
Laura Pullman, New York
February 10 2019, 12:01am, 
Marianne Williamson has joined the race for the 2020 Democratic presidential nomination
Countless Americans have fruitlessly prayed for Oprah Winfrey to relinquish her television throne and run for president. Now they have second best: the media mogul’s spiritual guru is taking a shot at toppling Donald Trump.
In another surreal moment for American politics, Marianne Williamson, the author of self-help books such as A Course in Weight Loss: 21 Spiritual Lessons for Surrendering Your Weight Forever, has joined the increasingly crowded race for the 2020 Democratic presidential nomination.
Sitting in her Manhattan living room — in a 20th-floor apartment with prime views of Trump Tower — the former cabaret singer explains: “My campaign is an effort to inspire greater love for political purposes.”
